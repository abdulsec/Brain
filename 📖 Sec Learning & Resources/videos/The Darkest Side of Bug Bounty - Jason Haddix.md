**Notes for *The Darkest Side of Bug Bounty* - Jason Haddix**  
#defcon #bugbounty #darkside #talks  

A look at the worst-case scenarios in bug bounty.

- **Program Practices**: Some programs are using attacker traffic to train AI models, build threat intel feeds, and improve scanners, resulting in no payments to hunters.
- **Cloud WAF Monitoring**: Cloud WAFs like Akamai track top bug bounty performers globally.
- **Triage Challenges**: Platform triage teams make life difficult for hackers by downgrading severity or marking valid reports as "informative" or "duplicate." In the worst cases, they even steal bugs from hunters and submit them to other programs.
- **Program Budgets**: Budgets are set annually, often limiting payouts.
- **Celebrity Bias**: Big-name hackers receive special services like out-of-band or instant support, while lesser-known hunters get nothing and sometimes face triage bias and burnout.
