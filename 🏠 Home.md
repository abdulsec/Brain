## Workflow

1. **Capture** anything new → `00 📥 Inbox` (don't overthink).
2. **Daily note** for today's todos, journal, scratch.
3. **Weekly review** — empty Inbox into the right topic folder.
4. Promote a topic note to a **Project** (`02 🎯 Projects`) when it has a goal + deadline.
